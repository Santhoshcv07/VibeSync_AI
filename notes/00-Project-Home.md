# VibeSync AI — Project Home

> Your mood. Your time. Your universe.

## Project Vision

VibeSync AI is an AI-powered entertainment discovery platform that creates personalized entertainment sessions called Vibes.

A Vibe is generated from:

- Mood
- Current activity
- Available time
- Energy level
- Entertainment preferences
- Previous feedback

A generated Vibe may include:

- Music
- YouTube videos
- Visual moodboards
- Time-aware movie recommendations
- Books
